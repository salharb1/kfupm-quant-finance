Before you start

Get your foundations solid

The MQF program assumes comfort with college calculus, statistics, and basic programming. Spend your pre-enrollment weeks refreshing these — they appear in every course.

∫ Math refresher

  • Linear algebra: matrices, eigenvalues
  • Optimization & Lagrange multipliers

Standout resources

Books, courses and channels worth bookmarking for the whole program.

📚 Core textbooks

  • Hull — Options, Futures & Other Derivatives (derivatives, fixed income)
  • Shreve — Stochastic Calculus for Finance I & II
  • Tsay — Analysis of Financial Time Series (econometrics)
  • Bodie, Kane & Marcus — Investments
  • Berk & DeMarzo — Corporate Finance

🎥 Channels & full courses

  • MIT 18.S096 — Mathematics in Finance
  • QuantPy — Python for quant finance
  • Patrick Boyle — markets & derivatives
  • Ben Lambert — econometrics

🧮 Tools to learn

  • Python (NumPy, pandas, statsmodels, scikit-learn)
  • Excel / VBA for quick modelling
  • R for econometrics
  • Bloomberg / Refinitiv terminals (on campus)
